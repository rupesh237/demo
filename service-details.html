Professional Custom Software Development

Our custom software development solutions are designed to meet your unique business needs and drive growth through innovation and technology.

Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

At MB Soft Tech, we follow a structured approach to software development to ensure high quality and successful project delivery. Our SDLC process includes the following six steps:

  • Planning: This initial phase involves defining the project scope, objectives, and constraints. We gather requirements from stakeholders and conduct feasibility studies to ensure the project is viable and aligned with business goals.
  • Analysis: In this phase, we analyze the gathered requirements in detail and create a detailed system specification. This step ensures we understand the user needs and system requirements thoroughly before proceeding.
  • Design: The design phase involves creating the architecture of the software solution. We design both the high-level and detailed components, including databases, user interfaces, and system interfaces, to ensure the solution meets all specified requirements.
  • Development: During development, our skilled developers write the code based on the design specifications. We follow best coding practices and use modern technologies to ensure the software is robust, scalable, and maintainable.
  • Testing: This crucial phase involves rigorous testing to identify and fix any defects or issues. We perform various types of testing, including unit testing, integration testing, system testing, and user acceptance testing, to ensure the software functions correctly and meets all requirements.
  • Deployment and Maintenance: Once the software passes all testing phases, we deploy it to the production environment. Post-deployment, we provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure the software remains functional, secure, and up-to-date.

By following these structured steps, we deliver custom software solutions that meet our clients' needs and drive their business success.
